The Data Science Institute Industry Affiliates (IA) Program connects leading companies with Temple University's data science research community. Industry partners engage directly with faculty and students, access cutting-edge research, and collaborate on real-world problems.

**IA Program Director**

**Become a Member**

Membership in the DSI Industry Affiliates Program offers:

* Establish industry-and-academia relationships
* Be the first to know about new research and results
* Get exposed to new methods and techniques
* Work on real-world problems with faculty and students
* Evaluate employment opportunities

For more information about joining the IA Program, contact [dsi@temple.edu](mailto:dsi@temple.edu).
