The Data Science Institute (DSI) at Temple University is the home to cutting-edge research in data science and analytics education. Housed in the Fox School of Business, the DSI focuses on a wide range of challenging methodological and applied problems in statistics, computer science, and business disciplines.

Faculty in the Data Science Institute work closely with private corporations, non-profits, and government organizations to identify important problems and to collaboratively develop sophisticated yet practical solutions that are poised to have a substantial impact in the world.

Scholars and practitioners at the DSI develop new lines of scientific inquiry, serve as a brain trust for the Greater Philadelphia Metropolitan Area, and function as a bridge for translating research and ideas into knowledge, products, and best practice for industry, government, and society at large.

Featured research areas include:

  1. Statistical methodology for large-scale data analysis
  2. Machine learning and artificial intelligence
  3. Network data science and causal inference
  4. Data-driven decision making in business and policy
  5. Applications in healthcare, social sciences, and finance

Industry partners include the Global Equities Division at Wells Fargo Securities, the Market Optimization Algorithms group at Google, and others. Learn more on the Affiliates page.
