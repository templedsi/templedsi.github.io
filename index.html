The Data Science Institute (DSI) at Temple University is a hub for interdisciplinary data science research, education, and innovation. Housed in the Fox School of Business, the DSI brings together faculty, students, and industry partners to advance the frontiers of data science and its applications.

Our mission is to develop rigorous methodology, drive impactful research, and train the next generation of data scientists to tackle real-world problems in business, healthcare, the social sciences, and beyond.

Current areas of focus include:

  1. Statistical methodology for large-scale data analysis
  2. Machine learning and artificial intelligence
  3. Network data science and causal inference
  4. Data-driven decision making in business and policy
  5. Applications in healthcare, social sciences, and finance

We collaborate with industry leaders and government agencies to translate research into practice, and we offer educational programs at the undergraduate, graduate, and executive levels.

Our work is supported by the National Science Foundation (NSF), the National Institutes of Health (NIH), and industry partners including Google, Microsoft, and others.
