**Date:** November 2, 2023  
**Location:** Fox School of Business, Temple University, Philadelphia, PA  
**Organizers:** Data Science Institute, Fox School of Business, Temple University & Wells Fargo Equity Finance

---

The third annual Temple – Wells Fargo Data Science Conference explored new frontiers in statistical machine learning and artificial intelligence, with sessions on network time series, natural language processing, and the evolving relationship between statistics, data science, and AI—all in the context of data-driven investing and systematic finance.

**Program**

| Session | Speaker(s) | Affiliation |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Opening Remarks | John Leone, Edoardo Airoldi, Chip Hunter | Wells Fargo; Temple University |
| Euclidean Mirrors for Discovering Dynamics in Network Time Series | Carey E. Priebe | Johns Hopkins University |
| Panel: Statistical Machine Learning and AI Tools for Complex Systems | Jianqing Fan, Betsy Ogburn, Alexander Volfovsky | Princeton; Johns Hopkins; Duke |
| Panel: Industry and University Collaboration — Rewards and Challenges | David Banks, James Rosenberger, Recai Yucel | Duke; NISS/Penn State; Temple |
| Natural Language Processing and Alternative Data Sources | David Blei | Columbia University |
| Panel: Statistics, Data Science, and AI — Are There Differences? Do They Matter? | Donald Rubin, Ed George, David Banks | Harvard/Temple; UPenn; Duke |
