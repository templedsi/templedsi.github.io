**Date:** November 10, 2021  
**Location:** The Union League of Philadelphia, Philadelphia, PA  
**Organizers:** Data Science Institute, Fox School of Business, Temple University & Wells Fargo Equity Finance

---

The inaugural Temple – Wells Fargo Data Science Conference brought together leading faculty from top research universities to discuss cutting-edge topics in statistical machine learning, deep learning, and artificial intelligence, with particular emphasis on their impact on systematic investing and quantitative finance.

**Program**

| Session | Speaker | Affiliation |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Opening Remarks | Ron Anderson, Edoardo Airoldi | Fox School of Business, Temple University |
| Modern Tools for Quantitative Investing | Jun Liu | Harvard University |
| Scalable Bayesian Inference | Natesh Pillai | Harvard University |
| Causal Inference for Sequential Decisions | Panos Toulis | University of Chicago, Booth School of Business |
| Bayesian Variable Selection and Prediction | Ed George | Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania |
| Dynamic Regression Models | Hedibert Freitas Lopes | Arizona State University & INSPER |
| Causal Inference in Complex Systems | Alexander Volfovsky | Duke University |
| Deep Learning: Foundations and Applications | Ruslan Salakhutdinov | Carnegie Mellon University |
| Data Science for Policy and Business | Alan Karr | Fraunhofer USA Center & NISS |
| Discussion | Donald Rubin | Harvard University emeritus & Temple University |
