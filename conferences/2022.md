**Date:** November 10, 2022  
**Organizers:** Data Science Institute, Fox School of Business, Temple University & Wells Fargo Equity Finance

---

The second annual Temple – Wells Fargo Data Science Conference continued to explore statistical machine learning, deep learning, and artificial intelligence, with a focus on applications in systematic investing and collaborative research between academia and industry.

**Program**

| Session | Speaker | Affiliation |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Opening Remarks | Ron Anderson, Jon Leone | Fox School of Business, Temple University |
| Bayesian Forecasting and Decisions in Finance and Economics | Mike West | Duke University |
| Explainable AI by Design via Semantic Information Pursuit | René Vidal | Johns Hopkins University |
| Panel: Innovative Applications of Statistics, ML, and AI | Ed George, Tian Zheng | University of Pennsylvania; Columbia University |
| Panel: Advances in Time Series for Systematic Trading | Rong Chen, Ken McAlinn, Jun Liu | Rutgers University; Temple University; Harvard University |
| Panel: Collaborative Learning and Research – Opportunities and Challenges | Donald B. Rubin, Ed George, Mike West | Harvard/Temple; UPenn; Duke |
