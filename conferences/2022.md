**Date:** November 10, 2022  
**Organizers:** Data Science Institute, Fox School of Business, Temple University & Wells Fargo Equity Finance

---

The second annual Temple – Wells Fargo Data Science Conference explored aspects of statistical machine learning, deep learning and AI with an emphasis on tools and ideas that are relevant to systematic investing methodologies.
